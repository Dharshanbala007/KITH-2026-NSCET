🧩 Visi-Verse

Teams are shown a series of images that together hint at a hidden word — it can be a movie, song, object, place, or anything. Decode the visual puzzle and guess the answer!

Rules & Regulations
  • Maximum 2 members per team.
  • No mobile phones allowed.
  • Answers must be called out clearly.
  • Multiple rounds with increasing difficulty.
  • Judges' decision is final.

Team Size: 1–2 Members

📋 Registration Deadline: April 7, 2026 🗓️ Symposium Date: April 10, 2026
🤔 Synchro-Speak

One member holds an image card above their head facing outward without seeing it — showing a person, place, or thing. Their partner gives verbal clues to help them guess what it is. Race against the clock!

Rules & Regulations
  • Exactly 2 members required.
  • Clue-giver cannot say the word, spell it, or use rhymes.
  • No gestures allowed — verbal clues only.
  • Time limit per round: 90 seconds.
  • Judges' decision is final.

Team Size: Exactly 2 Members

🎮 E-Sports — Free Fire

Battle it out in Free Fire duo mode! Team up with your partner and fight your way to victory in this intense battle royale competition.

Mode of Conduct

• Matches will be conducted in Duo mode
• Tournament bracket format
• Participants MUST bring their own mobile device with Free Fire installed and fully charged
• Ensure your device is charged and game is updated before arriving at the venue

Rules & Regulations
  • Exactly 2 members per team (Duo mode only)
  • Free Fire game only — no other game permitted
  • Participants must bring their own device (mobile phone) with Free Fire installed
  • Device must be fully charged before the match
  • Organisers will NOT provide any devices
  • Hacking, modding, or using any cheat tools is strictly prohibited and leads to immediate disqualification
  • No offensive language or unsportsmanlike behavior
  • Judges' / organizers' decision is final
  • Both members must be present at the venue
  • Teams must report 15 minutes before their match

Team Size: Exactly 2 members (Duo)
