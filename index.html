📄 Paper Presentation
Topic: "Engineering Innovations for Sustainable Development"

Students present technical research on engineering topics using PowerPoint.

Mode of Conduct

Submit abstract before event. Present using PPT (Max 8 slides).

Rules & Regulations
  • Technical/Engineering related content only.
  • Time: 6-7 min presentation + 2 min Q&A.
  • No plagiarism allowed.
  • Judges' decision is final.

Team Size: 1–2 Members

🖼️ Poster Presentation
Topic: "Engineering Innovations for Sustainable Development"

Visual storytelling of engineering solutions for sustainable development.

Mode of Conduct

Prepare A1 size printed poster. Explain to judges for 3-5 min.

Rules & Regulations
  • Poster must be original and creative.
  • A1 size (printed or handmade).
  • Max explanation time: 5 minutes.
  • Visual clarity and data accuracy are key.

Team Size: 1–2 Members

📋 Registration Deadline: April 9, 2026 🗓️ Symposium Date: April 10, 2026
🔌 Circuit Debugging

Identify and correct errors in electronic circuits or diagrams.

Mode of Conduct

Provided with faulty circuit diagrams or breadboard circuits to fix.

Rules & Regulations
  • No mobile phones or external help allowed.
  • Time limit: 20-30 minutes.
  • Team discussion is allowed within the team.
  • Accuracy and speed are the primary criteria.

Team Size: 1–2 Members

📋 Registration Deadline: April 9, 2026 🗓️ Symposium Date: April 10, 2026
💻 Code-a-thon

Solve programming problems within a time limit using C, Python, or Java.

Mode of Conduct

Solve 3-5 coding problems on the provided systems.

Rules & Regulations
  • Restricted internet access (only specific documentation).
  • Allowed languages: C, Python, Java.
  • No copying or external communication.
  • Efficiency of code and time taken will be judged.

Team Size: 1–2 Members

📋 Registration Deadline: April 9, 2026 🗓️ Symposium Date: April 10, 2026
🚀 Mini Startup

Pitch innovative startup ideas or product concepts to a panel of judges.

Mode of Conduct

Pitch startup idea using PPT or physical charts/models.

Rules & Regulations
  • Idea must be original and feasible.
  • Business model and target audience explanation required.
  • Time: 5-7 minutes for pitching.
  • Q&A session with judges follows the pitch.

Team Size: 2–4 Members

📋 Registration Deadline: April 9, 2026 📎 PPT Submission: April 9, 2026 🗓️ Symposium Date: April 10, 2026
⏱️ JAM (Just A Minute)

Speak on a given topic for one minute without hesitation or repetition.

Mode of Conduct

Pick a topic randomly from a bowl and speak for 1 minute.

Rules & Regulations
  • No hesitation, repetition, or stuttering.
  • No deviation from the topic.
  • Strictly 1 minute duration.
  • Language should be clear and professional.

Team Size: Individual

📋 Registration Deadline: April 9, 2026 🗓️ Symposium Date: April 10, 2026
🌟 Find the Talent

Showcase hidden talents like singing, dancing, magic, or any unique skill.

Mode of Conduct

Perform on stage for 2-3 minutes in front of judges.

Rules & Regulations
  • Time limit: Strictly 3 minutes.
  • No vulgarity or offensive content.
  • Background music must be submitted before the deadline.
  • Participants must bring their own props.

Team Size: Individual
